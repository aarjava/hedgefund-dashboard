## 2026-01-31 - Tooltips for Financial Parameters
**Learning:** Users (even experts) can misinterpret unit conventions (e.g. basis points vs decimals) or statistical thresholds (e.g. quantiles). Explicit examples in tooltips prevent errors.
**Action:** Always add `help` tooltips with concrete examples (e.g. "10 bps = 0.10%") to inputs involving financial units or statistical parameters.
