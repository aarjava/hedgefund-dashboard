## 2024-05-22 - Tooltips for Quantitative Parameters
**Learning:** Complex domain-specific parameters (like "Volatiltiy Quantile" or "Basis Points") are major friction points for non-expert users. Adding simple `help` tooltips can significantly lower the barrier to entry without cluttering the UI.
**Action:** Always add `help` tooltips to inputs that involve statistical thresholds, financial jargon, or advanced configuration.
