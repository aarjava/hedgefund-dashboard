## 2024-05-23 - Explaining Financial Jargon
**Learning:** In specialized apps (finance), users often struggle with acronyms like "bps" or statistical terms like "quantiles". Tooltips are critical here, not just for "how to use this widget" but "what does this concept mean".
**Action:** Always check input labels for industry jargon and add explanatory tooltips with concrete examples (e.g., "10 bps = 0.10%").
