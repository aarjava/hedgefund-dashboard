## 2025-05-20 - Tooltips for Financial Parameters
**Learning:** Quantitative interfaces often mix common terms (like 'date') with niche jargon ('volatility quantile', 'bps'). Users need contextual help inline for the latter to make the tool accessible to non-experts without cluttering the UI.
**Action:** Audit all specialized parameters (sliders, number inputs) and ensure `help` arguments provide clear definitions or examples.
