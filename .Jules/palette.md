## 2025-01-20 - Adding Tooltips to Financial Parameters
**Learning:** Users, especially non-experts, can be easily confused by financial acronyms like 'bps' or statistical concepts like 'quantile'.
**Action:** Always add a `help` tooltip to `st.slider` or `st.number_input` when the parameter involves domain-specific jargon. This simple addition significantly reduces cognitive load and improves accessibility without cluttering the UI.
