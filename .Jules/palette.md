## 2026-03-01 - Add Concrete Examples to Financial Parameter Tooltips
**Learning:** Users can find financial or statistical inputs (like basis points, quantiles, betas) ambiguous when only raw numerical values are used. Adding concrete examples to tooltips (e.g., "10 bps = 0.10%" or "0.75 = 75th percentile") significantly improves UX by confirming the expected format and meaning of the parameter.
**Action:** When adding inputs for domain-specific metrics (financial, statistical), always provide a tooltip with a concrete mapping/example of the value.
