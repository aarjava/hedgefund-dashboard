## 2025-03-01 - Concrete Examples in Statistical Parameter Inputs
**Learning:** Adding concrete examples to inputs involving statistical or financial parameters (e.g., basis points, quantiles) significantly improves user understanding and reduces input errors. Users may struggle with abstract units (like "bps" or "quantile") without direct context.
**Action:** When creating forms or settings panels that use specialized financial or statistical units, always use the `help` or tooltip property to provide a concrete, calculated example (e.g., "10 bps = 0.10%" or "0.75 = 75th percentile").
