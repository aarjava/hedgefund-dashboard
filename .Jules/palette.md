## 2024-05-24 - Context Tooltips for Financial Parameters
**Learning:** Inputs involving statistical or financial parameters (e.g., quantiles, basis points) must include `help` tooltips containing concrete examples to be accessible and intuitive.
**Action:** Always verify that complex parameters like `st.slider` and `st.number_input` have clear, contextual `help` arguments (e.g., "10 bps = 0.10%").
