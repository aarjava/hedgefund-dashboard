## 2026-03-05 - Add concrete examples to statistical and financial parameters via tooltips
**Learning:** Statistical and financial parameters like basis points or percentiles are often ambiguous to users. Adding concrete examples in tooltips significantly improves clarity and reduces errors.
**Action:** When adding inputs involving statistical or financial parameters (e.g., quantiles, basis points), include `help` tooltips containing concrete examples (e.g., "10 bps = 0.10%").
